From the record store to the main stage


5 years ago amongst the stacks of vinyl at High Fidelity Records, Blaire & Kenny were introduced by mutual friends, while Family of the Year played in the background. Since then they’ve moved up the California coast and back south again. They’ve collected a small zoo of rescue animals. And they’ve visited many more record stores and caught tons of shows along the way. On March 31st, 2018 they will mark the next big moment in their journey together with a wedding at one of Los Angeles’ most iconic music venues (appropriate, right?), The Fonda Theatre. Keep your calendars clear and your eyes open for formal invites coming soon. While you’re on the edge of your seats waiting for the invite, please make sure Blaire & Kenny have your most up to date address, phone and email information.
